	George Brown College (GBC) is a well-regarded public college of applied arts
and technology located in downtown Toronto, Ontario. Established in 1967,
it offers a wide range of programs and has a strong focus on practical, 
hands-on learning. There are some programs that GBC offers;
	Programs: GBC offers over 170 full-time programs, including diplomas,
advanced diplomas, degrees, and postgraduate certificates. Fields of study
include arts, design, business, community services, early childhood education,
construction and engineering technologies, health sciences, hospitality and
culinary arts, and preparatory studies1.
