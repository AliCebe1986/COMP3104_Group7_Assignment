Football (Soccer) - Main Rules:
1. A match is played between two teams of 11 players each.
2. The game lasts for 90 minutes, split into two 45-minute halves.
3. A goal is scored when the ball crosses the goal line between the goalposts.
4. Players can use any part of their body except their hands and arms to move the ball (goalkeeper can use hands within the penalty area).
5. Offside rule: A player is offside if they are nearer to the opponent's goal line than both the ball and the second-last defender when the ball is passed to them.
6. Fouls include tripping, pushing, handball, and dangerous play, leading to free kicks or penalties.
7. A penalty kick is awarded for fouls inside the penalty area.
8. Yellow and red cards are given for misconduct. Two yellow cards result in a red card, and a red card leads to a player's ejection.
