Basketball - Main Rules:
1. A team consists of 5 players.
2. The game starts with a jump ball at the center of the court.
3. Points are scored by throwing the ball into the opponent's basket.
   - 2 points for a regular shot, 3 points for a shot behind the 3-point line, 1 point for a free throw.
4. The ball can be moved by passing or dribbling (bouncing the ball while moving).
5. A player cannot take more than two steps without dribbling the ball.
6. Personal fouls: Contact with an opponent is restricted (e.g., pushing, tripping).
7. After 5 team fouls in a quarter, the opponent gets free throws.
8. The game is played in four quarters, each lasting 12 minutes (NBA) or 10 minutes (FIBA).
9. The team with the most points at the end of the game wins.

Basketball - Additional Rules:
10. Shot clock rule: A team has 24 seconds (NBA) or 14 seconds (after an offensive rebound) to attempt a shot that hits the rim.
